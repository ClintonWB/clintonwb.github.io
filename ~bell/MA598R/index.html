Francisco Blanco-Silva's real analysis problems

These are very nice problem sets created by
Francisco Blanco-Silva (Math PhD 2007)
to help graduate students in the PhD program
at Purdue prepare for the written qualifier
exam on real analysis.

  • Abstract and Lebesgue Measure. The Cantor Set.
  • Riemann-Stieltjes Integrations. Functions of Bounded Variation.
  • Problems on Integration.
  • Lp Spaces.
  • Measurable Functions.
  • Advanced Problems

NOTE: The problems in the sets are color-coded.
A statement in blue means that the question can
be used to solve another more-involved question below.
