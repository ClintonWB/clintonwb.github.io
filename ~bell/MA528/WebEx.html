How to join a WebEx office hour

At the appointed hour, go to

https://purdue.webex.com/join/bell

The first time you try this, you'll be
prompted to add a WebEx plug-in to your
browser. This is a small program and the
download is quick and easy.

When setting up audio, I think it is
best to connect via your computer rather
than via phone dial-up. Click on the
audio/computer icon to do this. I have
found that I do not need a headset in
order to use my microphone.

Remember to mute your mic if you aren't
talking. There is a "raise your hand"
button you can use to get my attention
if things get crazy.

I usually share a Windows Journal window
with participants, but you can share your
screen with me and the participants too.
Just let me know if and when you want to
do that and I'll grant you permission (and
tell you how it's done). We can also share
a whiteboard for writing math formulas that's
very useful. Let me know if you want to try
this and I'll tell you how it's done.
