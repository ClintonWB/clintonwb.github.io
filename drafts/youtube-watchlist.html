Studson Studio

There are many good model, sculpting, and diorama channels that I watch sometimes - but Studson Studio is my favorite. With a clear and approchable explanation of methods, a clear eye for unexpected ponential in every object he sees, and a hillarious dry sense of humor, I could watch this channel for hours. Beyond the beautiful builds, one thing that also sets this channel apart is how often he highlights other creators - interesting projects, tutorials, or as sources for his ideas and techniques - in a community-minded way that I really appreciate.
